About FoodieAI

Your personal AI-powered recipe assistant

Our Mission

FoodieAI was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to make cooking easier, more accessible, and more enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, our AI-powered recipe generator helps you create delicious meals with whatever ingredients you have on hand or based on your specific dietary preferences.

The Technology Behind FoodieAI

FoodieAI leverages Google's powerful Gemini 2.0 Flash model to analyze ingredients, understand culinary principles, and generate creative, practical recipes. Our AI has been trained on thousands of recipes from around the world, allowing it to recommend diverse dishes that are not only delicious but also properly balanced.

Key Features

  • Recipe Generation by Ingredients: Input what you have in your pantry and let the AI do the rest.
  • Recipe Type Search: Find recipes based on cuisine, meal type, and dietary restrictions.
  • Nutritional Information: Get detailed nutritional breakdowns for every recipe.
  • Download & Share: Save recipes as PDFs or share them with friends and family.

Our Vision

We believe that cooking should be accessible to everyone. Our vision is to create a world where no one has to worry about what to cook next or whether they can accommodate dietary restrictions. With FoodieAI, we're putting the power of a professional chef in your pocket, helping you create memorable meals with ease.

Meet the Team

FoodieAI is developed by a passionate team of food lovers, AI enthusiasts, and software developers committed to revolutionizing the way people approach cooking.
